What Sets Us Apart

100% focused on radiology

We specialize exclusively in radiology image annotation, allowing us to go deep in modality-specific nuances like CT lung nodules, MRI brain structures, and X-ray fracture labeling.

Medical-Grade Annotation Quality

All annotations are performed or reviewed by experienced radiologists, ensuring clinical relevance, consistency, and traceability in every project.

Custom Pipelines for Your Needs

Whether you’re preparing data for regulatory submission, research publications, or AI model training, we tailor our workflow to meet your technical and compliance needs.

Transparent Audit Trails

Every annotation includes versioning, annotator ID, timestamps, and change logs—essential for reproducibility, quality assurance, and regulatory documentation. (only on MVA.ai platform)
